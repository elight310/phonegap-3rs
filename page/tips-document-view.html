Tips for Document View

  • Tap a document to open it.

  • Tap a folder to open it.

  • Tap a document name to change it.

  • Press and hold a document until all documents wiggle. When it's wiggling you can:

  • Make folders - drag a document on top of another.

  • Delete documents - select one or more documents then tap.

  • Duplicate documents - select one or more documents then tap <duplicate>.
